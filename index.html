Meet Dr. Sam

The doctor that makes gut health fun, accessible, and life-changing

Not Your Average Doctor

Dr. Sam combines cutting-edge medical expertise with a refreshing approach to gut health. After seeing thousands of patients struggle with digestive issues, Dr. Sam realized that the missing piece wasn't more medicine—it was making gut health engaging and fun.

With board certification in gastroenterology and a background in nutritional science, Dr. Sam brings both the scientific knowledge and the creative approach needed to transform your gut health journey.

Dr. Sam's philosophy is simple: your gut should work for you, not against you—and getting there should be an adventure, not a chore.
